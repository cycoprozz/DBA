The Heart of DBA

Deeply believing. Honestly agnostic.

It's not a contradiction — it's an invitation. The deepest faith doesn't demand certainty. It asks for presence.

Start Where You Are

No prerequisites. No conversion. No "you should believe this." Just a real conversation about your actual life — the beautiful parts, the hard parts, and everything in between.

For Every Human

Every background. Every identity. Every age. Every tradition — or none at all. If you're breathing and wondering, you belong here.

Room for Doubt & Faith

Bring your belief. Bring your doubt. Bring the anger you're afraid to admit you carry — or the hollow silence where certainty used to live. Bring your longing for meaning that won't quit, even when you wish it would. All of it fits here. None of it disqualifies you. The only requirement is showing up.

Who You're Talking To

David Rickey

For over forty years, I've sat with people in their most vulnerable moments — as a priest, as a psychotherapist, and simply as a fellow human being trying to make sense of it all.

I trained in psychoanalytic psychotherapy at the Blainton-Peale Graduate Institute and earned my Master of Divinity from Union Theological Seminary. Those two paths — the clinical and the spiritual — have never been separate to me. They're different languages for the same thing: paying attention to what's actually happening inside a person.

I served as Rector of Trinity+St. Peter's Episcopal Church in San Francisco. Now, in this new chapter, I'm creating the space I always wished existed — one where therapy and soul-work aren't kept in separate rooms, and where no one is turned away because they can't afford help.

✦ Why this is a nonprofit Good counseling is expensive. Most people who need someone to talk to can't afford $150–$250 per session. Insurance often doesn't cover it, or the deductible makes it impossible. I've watched too many people suffer in silence because the math didn't work. DBA exists so that money isn't the reason you don't get help. Sliding scale, always. No one turned away.

Humanitarian Work

Beyond the counseling room

DBA isn't just about sessions. A big part of this work is showing up for people who can't afford help — the unhoused, families struggling to eat, kids who need someone in their corner. Every dollar you give goes directly to food, supplies, and direct care. No overhead. No middleman. Just people helping people.

Feed Someone Today

Hot meals, grocery runs, and food drops for people living on the streets. Even a few dollars puts a real meal in front of someone who hasn't eaten.

Kids & Families

School supplies, winter coats, backpacks, and direct support for children and families who are barely holding on. Sometimes it's the smallest things that change everything.

Direct Supplies

Blankets, hygiene kits, socks, first-aid — the basics that make surviving the street just a little more bearable. We buy in bulk and distribute directly.
